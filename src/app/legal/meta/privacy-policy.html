Privacy Policy

Our app does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. It only requests and displays publicly available data from the Instagram oEmbed API to embed Instagram posts on our website.

We do not require user authentication, nor do we access any private Instagram information. The API response is used only for immediate rendering and is not stored or reused.

If you have any concerns or inquiries about privacy, please contact us at contact@beautylink.com.
