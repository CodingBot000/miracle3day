What is Mimotok?

Mimo, Beautiful Appearance
Mimo (미모) means "beautiful appearance" in Korean.
It represents healthy, radiant skin and harmonious facial features.
Talk → Tok
We added 'tok' from the pronunciation of 'talk'.
It embodies our vision: discussing beauty, sharing information, and connecting people.
Our Mission
Mimotok was created to introduce the excellence of K-beauty to the world
and provide trustworthy information about dermatology and cosmetic procedures.
Who We Are
Built through collaboration between dermatologists and developers,
Mimotok offers both medically verified information and seamless user experience.
Your journey to beauty starts here, with Mimotok.
