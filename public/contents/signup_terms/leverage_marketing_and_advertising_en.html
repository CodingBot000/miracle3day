Purposes of Collection and Processing

We process personal information to provide promotional information and opportunities to participate in events; deliver services and ads tailored to demographic characteristics; and send event notices.

Personal Information Collected

Name, mobile phone number, country of residence, email, nickname, gender, date of birth, interests, “automatically generated/collected information,” and AI analysis results

“Automatically generated/collected information”

  • Visit time, service‑use records, records of improper use, transaction records, access logs (browser type, operating system)
  • Content authored by the data subject, search terms, language
  • Cookies, IP address, MAC address, UICC‑ID, device identifiers (UDID, UUID), universal IC card identifier (UICC‑ID), advertising identifiers (ADID, IDFA), device information (OS, screen size, model name, manufacturer)

Retention and Use Period

Until membership withdrawal.

Right to Refuse Consent and Possible Disadvantages

You have the right to refuse consent to the collection and use of personal information; however, refusal may limit the provision of services corresponding to the stated purposes.
